How It Works

Four steps to mastery

UltraLearn turns every session into a precise diagnostic and learning event. Here's what happens from the moment you log in.

1
Diagnostic Assessment

A 20-minute adaptive placement test maps your exact knowledge state across every topic — not just what you know, but how confidently and efficiently you know it.

2
Personalized Session Build

Your session is assembled from 60,000+ questions in real time, weighted by your weak spots, preferred difficulty ramp, and cognitive fatigue window to maximize every minute.

3
Live Behavioral Tracking

Every move, pause, correction, and calculation path is captured. This data feeds directly into your learning model — making each session smarter than the last.

4
Insight Report & Loop

After each session you receive a detailed insight report: accuracy by concept, speed vs. mastery tradeoffs, identified misconceptions, and your personalized next-session plan.
